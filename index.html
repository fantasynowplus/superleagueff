HOW

How it works

Three steps between you and a starting lineup.

1. Register

Sign up and donate — 100% of proceeds support the league's charity partner, Extra Life.

2. Get placed

Once invited, pick an open division to get assigned to! Whether it's with friends or foes, the season will be a competitive one!

3. Draft & compete

Run your season like any other league — waivers & trash talk — while your entry does good.

WHY

Why we play

Super Leagueexists because fantasy football is more fun when the stakes mean something beyond bragging rights.

Every dollar in goes directly to the Children's Miracle Network Hospitals. This is a free league to join, but all donations go directly to Extra Life. League standings decide the ring — the donation total is the real scoreboard.
