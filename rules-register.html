Rules & Registration

Know the Rules. Lock your spot.

Read through the league format below, then register for your chance to compete in Super League.

Welcome to the Super League, where your love for fantasy football transforms into tangible support for children and their families. As you draft your team and strategize for victory, know that every touchdown and interception contributes to vital programs and resources. Join us in this thrilling tournament, where passion meets purpose, and together, we'll score big for those in need.

Draft Information

  • All #SLFF4 drafts will begin on Saturday, August 1st at 12pm EST
  • The draft will be on Sleeper and MyFantasyLeague
  • 8-hour pick timer
  • Overnight shutoff between 1am EST to 8am EST
  • The draft will be 14 rounds, snake style (No Third Round Reversal)
  • No draft pick trading

League Information

  • Format: Best Ball with Managed Waivers
  • Roster Start: 2 RB, 3 WR, 1 TE, 5 WRT, 2 WRTQ
  • Bench: 1 bench player
  • Trading: Not allowed
  • FAAB Budget: $200
  • Waivers: Run at Noon EST daily (Sunday – players go to free agency)
  • Scoring: Weekly game against league median

League Rules

Draft Timer

  • #SLFF4 commissioners have the right to replace any league member in the first round if they've been on the clock for more than 4 hours.
  • If the clock expires while you are on the clock, a player will be selected based on Sleeper ADP. Replacements won't be made unless the selection is completely unreasonable.
  • If you are on the turn, please make both picks within a reasonable time. Do not be the person who wastes 16 hours on the clock to make two picks.
  • The draft timer is to accommodate work and personal lives. If you are available to make a pick, you should make the pick within a reasonable time.
  • People have lives. After the first round, please contact a league commissioner if a consistent timeout issue or multiple timeouts are happening.

Accidental Drops / Picks

  • Accidental Drop: You must contact a league commissioner ASAP to ensure that the player is added back to your roster. Waiver drops will not be reversed.
  • Accidental Pick: You must contact a league commissioner immediately before the next draft pick is made. If picks are made, the commissioner will not be able to reverse the pick. Say in the chat that the wrong pick was made, so that others are aware and will refrain from making a pick.

Anti-Collusion

  • Players who are deemed as starting worthy players should not be dropped. If they are, they will be re-added to the roster.
  • Because there is only one bench spot this year, you may need to drop a startable player who is on bye or injured. This will not go against you when that happens.
  • Multiple violations will get a team locked for the remainder of the year.

Waiver Moves

  • Waivers will run daily at Noon EST starting Monday, September 1, 2025.
  • Teams that are eliminated from the playoffs will be locked from making additional moves.

Playoffs

Playoffs will begin in Week 15. The top 80 teams will make it to the playoffs and compete for most points possible each week. Eliminated teams will be locked to prevent add/drops in the final weeks. Teams that make the playoffs will have the opportunity to earn extra FAAB.
